Black hat hackers are individuals who engage in hacking activities with malicious intent, seeking to cause harm to individuals, organizations, or systems. White hat hackers, on the other hand, use their skills to identify vulnerabilities in systems and networks in order to help improve security measures. Grey hat hackers fall somewhere in between, using their skills for both ethical and unethical purposes.
